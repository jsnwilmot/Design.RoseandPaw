Custom website design for small businesses

Mobile-friendly website design with clear contact actions.

Custom small business websites that are mobile-friendly, fast-loading, easy to navigate, and built around clear contact actions. Good business website design should answer what you offer, where you work, what it costs, and how someone gets started.

  • Responsive website design for phones, tablets, laptops, and desktops
  • Service business website design and local business website design
  • Website redesign services, website updates, and website maintenance
  • Clean, professional design with no generic template look

Supporting service: basic SEO setup

Practical SEO basics built into the website.

Search engine optimization, often shortened to SEO, means setting up your website so search engines can better understand your pages. Basic SEO setup gives the site a stronger launch foundation: useful page titles, meta descriptions, headings, image alt text, internal links, clean URLs, service area wording, structured data, sitemap.xml, and robots.txt.

  • Small business SEO basics and local SEO foundations
  • Service area wording for Canada-wide or local businesses
  • Technical basics for crawlable, organized static websites
  • Honest recommendations with no ranking guarantees

Supporting service: business profile setup

Google and Yelp profiles that look complete and consistent.

Business profile setup gives customers more ways to verify the business before they contact you. Rose & Paw Digital Design can help set up or clean up Google Business Profile and Yelp Business Profile details so they match the website.

  • Categories, services, description, contact details, and service area
  • Profile cleanup for businesses with outdated listings
  • Local search optimization without keyword stuffing
  • Review link can be added once available

Print-ready and digital design that matches the website.

Graphic design support can include business card design, print-ready files, social media post design, Facebook post design, flyers, price lists, and service menus that feel consistent with the website.

  • Business card design with print-ready design files
  • Social media graphics for small business promotions
  • Flyers, service menus, and price list design
  • Brand-aligned files for online and print use
