Privacy Policy

Plain-language privacy information.

This page explains how Rose & Paw Digital Designs may handle information submitted through this website. It is not legal advice.

Information you choose to send

If you call, email, use the inquiry form, or contact Rose & Paw through Facebook, you may provide details such as your name, business name, email address, phone number, service interest, budget or package interest, and message.

Quote form submissions

The quote request form is processed through Web3Forms so the message can be delivered by email. Web3Forms may receive the details you enter into the form, including your contact information and project message.

How information may be used

Information may be used to respond to inquiries, prepare quotes, discuss website design or digital design services, schedule appointments, provide project support, and follow up about requested work.

Cookies and analytics

This website may use cookies and basic website analytics, including Google Analytics, to measure website traffic and understand visitor actions such as page views, contact clicks, and quote request interest. A real GA4 Measurement ID has not been added yet. Event tracking is coded so it does not run unless a valid Google tag is present and non-essential cookies have been accepted.

Google Ads tracking

Google Ads tracking may be used if advertising is enabled in the future. Non-essential analytics and marketing storage are set to denied by default unless a visitor accepts cookies through the website banner.

Third-party services and external links

Third-party services may include Web3Forms, Google Analytics, Google Ads if enabled, and external links such as Facebook, email, phone links, or other tools used to respond to inquiries. External websites have their own privacy practices.

No selling of personal information

Rose & Paw Digital Designs does not sell personal information.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to design@roseandpaw.ca.
